IM SO BORED

I get bored a lot and when I don't know what to do I go to the website My life is boring and I want to do a side quest but dont know what to do.

Listed below are some of the fun side quests it has generated for me.

  • Do a funny post (e.g., recreate a meme or a bro-pose) with a stranger and post it in your story
  • Speak to strangers with a foreign accent with your friends. First one to get caught loses
  • Go out (take the paint and whatever you're painting on with you) and paint anything you can see.
  • Set an alarm for 1 AM or 2 AM, specifically within that range, and do 20 push-ups and 15 sit-ups.

To make the white space below the footer go away I made the whole thing a grid. Then I set the rows to be auto, 1fr, auto. To make sure the footer went to the bottom of the page I set min hight to 100vh and to margins 0.
