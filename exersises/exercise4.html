Photo Tour of Italy

Italy is a beautiful European country bordering the Mediterranean ocean with gorgeous views of crystal clear water. Known for being the home of the Roman Empire and the Renaissance, unparalleled art, and iconic architecture like the Colosseum. Additionally their rich culture is enhanced by world-class cuisine like pizza, pasta, gelato, wine, and seafood. Here you will get a taste of a few of the main cities and tourist destinations within Italy’s diverse landscape.

Cinque terre, Italy

Cinque Terre is a string of five centuries-old seaside villages on the rugged Italian Riviera coastline. Known for their colorful houses and vineyards cling to steep terraces, there are harbors filled with fishing boats and trattorias turn out seafood specialties along with the Liguria region’s famous sauce, pesto. This is a must see destination on your next trip to Italy.

Sorento, Italy

Eight hours south down the coast from Cinque Terre you will find Sorento, a coastal town in southwestern Italy, facing the Bay of Naples on the Sorrentine Peninsula. It’s known for sweeping water views and Piazza Tasso, a cafe-lined square. The historic center is a warren of narrow alleys that's home to the Chiesa di San Francesco, a 14th-century church with a tranquil cloister. This would be a relaxing stop on your tour of Italy.

Venice, Italy

Back up on the northeastern coast is Venice, the City of Canals. Built on 118 small islands connected by over 400 bridges in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea. It is famous for romantic gondola rides, the Grand Canal, and stunning, unique architecture like St. Mark's Basilica and the Doge’s Palace.
