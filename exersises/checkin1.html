Olympic Standings 2026: Alpine Ski Racing

Alpine ski racing has been part of the Olympic Winter Games for 90 years, debuting at the 1936 Games in Garmisch-Partenkirchen, Germany. Initially athletes participated in the just the combined event, but today the list has expanded to include individual events.

Events

There are 5 events that athletes can participate in at the winter olympics.

  1. Sl - Slalom
  2. GS - Giant Slalom
  3. SG - Super-G
  4. DH - Downhill
  5. Team Combined - Slalom and Super-G

American Results

The Americans did well in this year taking home medals in 4 different events.

  • Breezy Johnson: Gold in Women's Downhill
  • Mikaela Shiffrin: Gold in Women's Slalom
  • Paula Moltzan & Jacqueline Wiles: Bronze in Women'sTeam Combined
  • Ryan Cochran-Siegle: Silver in Men's Super-G
